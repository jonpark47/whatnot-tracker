How this works

Before the show: stick a colored dot sticker on each card matching its owner's color below (no numbers yet). During the show: when a card sells, tap that person's color button on the Live Tracker tab — it assigns the next sale number automatically. After the show: paste the Whatnot export on the Reconcile tab to calculate everyone's totals, fees, and the reserve.

Fees & Reserve

Defaults match Whatnot's published standard US rate as of July 2026: 8% commission (5% for electronics, 4% for coins/currency - check your Seller Dashboard if you sell those) plus 2.9% + $0.30 payment processing per order. Fees can change or vary by promo, so double check against your own payout statement and adjust here if needed.
